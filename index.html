About the Artist

Webin Comics is an original webcomic series crafted by Samantha Aleria, a passionate small artist with a big love for storytelling through art. Known for her bold creativity and ever-evolving style, Samantha blends multiple art styles, experiments with techniques, and brings her favorite manhwa inspirations to life on every page. Each episode of Webin Comics is a playful yet skillful exploration—where reference studies meet original storytelling, and where fan-favorite manhwas are honored through unique reinterpretations and visual tributes. From expressive character designs to dynamic paneling, Samantha uses her comics as both a sketchbook and a stage—always learning, always growing, and always creating. Whether you're a fellow art enthusiast, a manhwa fan, or just someone who loves watching passion turn into pages, Webin Comics offers a colorful, heartfelt glimpse into the creative world of an artist who dares to mix, match, and make it her own.
